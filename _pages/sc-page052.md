## ARGUMENT.  

*Subject proposed. Address to William Shenstone, Esq. Of monkeys.  
Of rats and other vermin. Of weeds. Of the yellow fly. Of  
the greasy fly. Of the blast. A hurricane described. Of calms  
and earthquakes. A tale.*
